**Architecture** is such a loaded word. 

It can be applied to everything in our world and describes the _structure_ that holds an idea, principle, system, process, or organization together. It is all the _contracts_ (agreements) between all parts to form a foundation. 

Walls are the parts that defines the structure (architecture). But the room is the function. The furniture is the use case. Structure determines what is possible.
